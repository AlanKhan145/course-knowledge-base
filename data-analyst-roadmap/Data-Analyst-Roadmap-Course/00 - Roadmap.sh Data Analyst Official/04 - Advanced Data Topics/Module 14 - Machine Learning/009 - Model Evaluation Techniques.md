## 3. Khai niem chinh

- Evaluation do model co tong quat hoa tot hay khong.
- Metric phu thuoc bai toan: MAE/RMSE cho regression, precision/recall/F1/AUC cho classification.
- Can tach train/test va tranh leakage.
