## 1. Tom tat

Bai nay giai thich **Hypothesis Testing** trong boi canh Data Analyst hien dai. Sau bai hoc, ban nen biet topic nay giup tra loi cau hoi data nao, nam o dau trong workflow va co the bien thanh query, spreadsheet, notebook, chart, dashboard hoac portfolio artifact.

## 2. Muc tieu hoc tap

- Giai thich duoc Hypothesis Testing bang ngon ngu cua ban.
- Nhan biet topic nay nam o dau trong workflow data analyst.
- Ap dung vao mot dataset, query, spreadsheet, notebook, chart hoac dashboard nho.

## 3. Khai niem chinh

- Hypothesis testing giup danh gia ket qua co kha nang do ngau nhien hay khong.
- Can hieu null hypothesis, p-value, confidence interval va sample size.
- Dung test sai co the tao ket luan sai du data co ve thuyet phuc.

## 4. Vi du / Demo

```text
H0: campaign A and B have the same conversion rate
H1: campaign B has a different conversion rate
Check: sample size, p-value, confidence interval, business impact
```

## 5. Bai tap thuc hanh

- Tinh metric tren toan bo dataset va theo it nhat 2 segment.
- Ve chart kiem tra distribution hoac relationship.
- Viet 3 cau ket luan kem caveat ve sample/data quality.

## 6. Loi thuong gap

- Ket luan qua nhanh khi chua xem sample size va distribution.
- Danh dong correlation voi causation.
- Chi nhin average ma bo qua segment va outlier.

## 7. Checklist hoan thanh

- Toi co the giai thich **Hypothesis Testing** trong 1-2 phut.
- Toi co mot vi du nho hoac artifact thuc hanh cho bai nay.
- Toi biet topic nay lien quan den dataset, metric, chart, insight hoac business question nao.
- Toi da ghi lai it nhat mot caveat hoac cau hoi can phan tich tiep.

## 8. Outcome lien quan

Analyze relationships between variables, test hypotheses and reason from data instead of guesswork.

## 9. Tong ket
