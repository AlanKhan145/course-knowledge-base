- Ap dung vao mot artifact nho: note, diagram, Solidity code, test, deploy script, dApp UI, audit finding hoac README.

## 3. Khai niem chinh

- Decentralization reduces dependence on one trusted operator.
- It usually trades simplicity and speed for censorship resistance, transparency and shared control.
- A product should use blockchain only when these tradeoffs are worth it.
