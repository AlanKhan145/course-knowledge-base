- Ap dung vao mot artifact nho: note, diagram, Solidity code, test, deploy script, dApp UI, audit finding hoac README.

## 3. Khai niem chinh

- Foundry is a fast Solidity-focused toolkit with forge, cast and anvil.
- It supports Solidity-native tests, fuzzing, scripting and gas snapshots.
- It is excellent for developers who want a contract-first workflow.
