## 3. Khai niem chinh

- An iOS Developer builds apps for Apple's mobile ecosystem using Swift, Xcode, SDK frameworks and platform design patterns.
- The role includes UI, app architecture, networking, persistence, testing, debugging and release work.
- A strong junior iOS developer can ship a small app end to end and explain the tradeoffs behind the implementation.
