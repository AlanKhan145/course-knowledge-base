## Learning Objectives

By the end of this lecture, students should understand:

* The ten automations cover: sprint tracker, morning brief, market pulse, research team, personal CRM, meeting intel, email triage, expense wrangler, content machine, and weekly executive reporting.
* Each automation builds on vault setup and tool connections from earlier modules.
* Automations can be run individually or chained.
* Priority order: start with high-frequency daily workflows.
