# 142 - Build Market Pulse Automation

## Section

Automation Blueprints Part 1

## Main Idea

Create a market and news monitoring automation that tracks relevant developments.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this lecture, students should understand:

* Define the market pulse scope: specific markets, topics, or companies.
* Write automation instructions for news and market data gathering.
* Format outputs as a concise market summary.
* Connect to news and market data sources.
* Test with current market data.
