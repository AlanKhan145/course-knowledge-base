## Section

## Main Idea

Create a multi-agent research workflow that produces comprehensive research outputs.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this lecture, students should understand:

* Define the research team: researcher, analyst, writer, reviewer roles.
* Write automation instructions for each role.
* Set up handoffs between team members.
* Test with a real research question.
* Review output quality and accuracy.
