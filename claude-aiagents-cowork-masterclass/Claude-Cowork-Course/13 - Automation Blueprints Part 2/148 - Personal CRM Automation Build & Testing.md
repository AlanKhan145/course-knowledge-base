# 148 - Personal CRM Automation Build & Testing

## Section

Automation Blueprints Part 2

## Main Idea

Automate relationship tracking and follow-up management with a personal CRM automation.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this lecture, students should understand:

* Define CRM inputs: contact list, interaction history, follow-up schedule.
* Write automation instructions for relationship status tracking.
* Connect to email and calendar for interaction data.
* Test the CRM with real contacts.
* Validate follow-up generation quality.
