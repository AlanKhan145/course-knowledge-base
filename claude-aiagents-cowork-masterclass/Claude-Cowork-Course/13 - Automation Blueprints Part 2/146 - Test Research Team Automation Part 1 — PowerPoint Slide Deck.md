## Main Idea

Test the research team automation by generating a PowerPoint slide deck from research findings.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this lecture, students should understand:

* Run the research team on a specific topic.
* Ask the team to format findings as a slide deck.
* Review the slide content for accuracy and structure.
* Refine the automation instructions based on output quality.
* Save the tested workflow as a template.
