# 132 - Personal AI Agent Setup Part 1: Share Your Social Media, CV, Goals & Writeups

## Section

Personal AI Agent Setup Process

## Main Idea

Add personal context, CV, goals, and writing samples to the agent vault.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this lecture, students should understand:

* Export or copy your LinkedIn profile or resume into the profile folder.
* Document your current goals in the goals folder.
* Add recent writing samples to the vault.
* Include your social media bio and key posts.
* Review all content for accuracy and relevance.
