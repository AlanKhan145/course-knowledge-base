## Main Idea

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this lecture, students should understand:

* Specify genre, audience, tone, and length in the initial prompt.
* Use follow-up prompts to develop specific scenes or characters.
* Ask Claude to revise for a different tone or reading level.
* Treat output as a first draft to be refined, not a final product.
