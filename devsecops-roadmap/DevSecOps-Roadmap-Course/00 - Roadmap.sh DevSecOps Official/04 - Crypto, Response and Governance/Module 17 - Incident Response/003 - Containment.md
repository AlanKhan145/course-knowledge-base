## 1. Tom tat

Bai nay giai thich **Containment** trong boi canh DevSecOps hien dai. Sau bai hoc, ban nen biet topic nay giam risk nao, nam o dau trong lifecycle va co the ap dung vao code, pipeline, cloud, runtime hay incident response.

## 2. Muc tieu hoc tap

- Giai thich duoc Containment bang ngon ngu cua ban.
- Nhan biet topic nay nam o dau trong DevSecOps lifecycle.
- Tao duoc mot artifact nho: checklist, script, config, scan, detection rule hoac runbook.

## 3. Khai niem chinh

- Containment gioi han tac dong bang disable account, rotate secret, block IP, isolate host hoac revoke token.
- Containment can can bang giua giam thiet hai va giu bang chung.
- Moi hanh dong containment nen duoc ghi log va co owner.

## 4. Vi du / Demo

```text
Signal -> triage -> severity -> containment -> evidence -> follow-up action
```

## 5. Bai tap thuc hanh

- Viet mot detection idea hoac runbook ngan cho topic nay.
- Xac dinh log source, owner, severity va first response action.
- Ghi lai evidence can thu thap khi co incident.

## 6. Loi thuong gap

- Alert qua nhieu false positive nen team bo qua tin hieu that.
- Khong co runbook, owner hoac severity ro rang.
- Khong giu evidence va timeline khi xu ly incident.

## 7. Checklist hoan thanh

- Toi co the giai thich **Containment** trong 1-2 phut.
- Toi co mot artifact nho hoac ghi chu thuc hanh cho bai nay.
- Toi biet topic nay lien quan den risk, control, evidence va owner nao.
- Toi da ghi lai it nhat mot cau hoi can tim hieu sau neu con mo ho.

## 8. Outcome lien quan

Prepare, identify, contain, recover and learn from security incidents.

## 9. Tong ket

**Containment** la mot moc trong lo trinh DevSecOps. Hay bien no thanh mot checklist, script, config, scan, detection rule, dashboard hoac runbook de kien thuc co cho bam.
