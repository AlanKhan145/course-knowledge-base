## 1. Tom tat

Bai nay giai thich **Risk Quantification** trong boi canh DevSecOps hien dai. Sau bai hoc, ban nen biet topic nay giam risk nao, nam o dau trong lifecycle va co the ap dung vao code, pipeline, cloud, runtime hay incident response.

## 2. Muc tieu hoc tap

- Giai thich duoc Risk Quantification bang ngon ngu cua ban.
- Nhan biet topic nay nam o dau trong DevSecOps lifecycle.
- Tao duoc mot artifact nho: checklist, script, config, scan, detection rule hoac runbook.

## 3. Khai niem chinh

- Risk quantification danh gia impact, likelihood, exposure va business value.
- No giup uu tien remediation thay vi chi sap xep theo severity ky thuat.
- Can noi risk bang ngon ngu business va engineering cung hieu.

## 4. Vi du / Demo

```text
Principal -> permission -> resource -> justification -> review date -> evidence
```

## 5. Bai tap thuc hanh

- Map topic nay voi mot control, evidence va owner.
- Ghi lai tan suat review va audit artifact can luu.
- Viet mot cau giai thich risk cho stakeholder khong phai engineer.

## 6. Loi thuong gap

- Lam compliance nhu checklist giay to nhung control khong thuc thi.
- Khong co evidence hoac evidence khong tai lap duoc.
- Khong noi duoc risk voi tac dong business.

## 7. Checklist hoan thanh

- Toi co the giai thich **Risk Quantification** trong 1-2 phut.
- Toi co mot artifact nho hoac ghi chu thuc hanh cho bai nay.
- Toi biet topic nay lien quan den risk, control, evidence va owner nao.
- Toi da ghi lai it nhat mot cau hoi can tim hieu sau neu con mo ho.

## 8. Outcome lien quan

Map controls to compliance frameworks and explain risk in business terms.

## 9. Tong ket

**Risk Quantification** la mot moc trong lo trinh DevSecOps. Hay bien no thanh mot checklist, script, config, scan, detection rule, dashboard hoac runbook de kien thuc co cho bam.
