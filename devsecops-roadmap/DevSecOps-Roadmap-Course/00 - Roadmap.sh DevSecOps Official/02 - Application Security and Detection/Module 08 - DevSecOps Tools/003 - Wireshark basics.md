## 1. Tom tat

Bai nay giai thich **Wireshark basics** trong boi canh DevSecOps hien dai. Sau bai hoc, ban nen biet topic nay giam risk nao, nam o dau trong lifecycle va co the ap dung vao code, pipeline, cloud, runtime hay incident response.

## 2. Muc tieu hoc tap

- Giai thich duoc Wireshark basics bang ngon ngu cua ban.
- Nhan biet topic nay nam o dau trong DevSecOps lifecycle.
- Tao duoc mot artifact nho: checklist, script, config, scan, detection rule hoac runbook.

## 3. Khai niem chinh

- Wireshark phan tich packet de debug protocol, TLS, DNS va network issue.
- Can biet filter, follow stream, handshake va cach an thong tin nhay cam.
- Packet capture nen co permission, thoi luong ngan va storage an toan.
