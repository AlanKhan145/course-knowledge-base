## 1. Tom tat

Bai nay giai thich **DNS** trong boi canh DevSecOps hien dai. Sau bai hoc, ban nen biet topic nay giam risk nao, nam o dau trong lifecycle va co the ap dung vao code, pipeline, cloud, runtime hay incident response.

## 2. Muc tieu hoc tap

- Giai thich duoc DNS bang ngon ngu cua ban.
- Nhan biet topic nay nam o dau trong DevSecOps lifecycle.
- Tao duoc mot artifact nho: checklist, script, config, scan, detection rule hoac runbook.

## 3. Khai niem chinh

- DNS bien domain thanh record va cung la diem tan cong nhu spoofing, takeover, tunneling.
- Can biet A, CNAME, MX, TXT, TTL, delegation va subdomain ownership.
- DevSecOps can audit record cu, subdomain bo quen va cau hinh SPF/DKIM/DMARC.
