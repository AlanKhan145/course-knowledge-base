## 1. Tom tat

Bai nay giai thich **HTTP** trong boi canh DevSecOps hien dai. Sau bai hoc, ban nen biet topic nay giam risk nao, nam o dau trong lifecycle va co the ap dung vao code, pipeline, cloud, runtime hay incident response.

## 2. Muc tieu hoc tap

- Giai thich duoc HTTP bang ngon ngu cua ban.
- Nhan biet topic nay nam o dau trong DevSecOps lifecycle.
- Tao duoc mot artifact nho: checklist, script, config, scan, detection rule hoac runbook.

## 3. Khai niem chinh

- HTTP la nen tang cua web/API voi method, status code, header, body va cache.
- Nhieu control security nam o header nhu CSP, HSTS, CORS va cookie flags.
- Debug HTTP can xem request path, auth header, status, redirect, timeout va proxy behavior.

- Viet 5 dong tom tat bai hoc khong nhin tai lieu.

- Tim mot loi production/security co the lien quan va viet cach debug.

## 6. Loi thuong gap

- Hoc thuoc dinh nghia nhung khong tao artifact thuc hanh.
- Bo qua scope, permission va data nhay cam khi dung security tool.
- Khong ghi lai cau hoi con mo de quay lai sau.

## 7. Checklist hoan thanh

- Toi co the giai thich **HTTP** trong 1-2 phut.
- Toi co mot artifact nho hoac ghi chu thuc hanh cho bai nay.
- Toi biet topic nay lien quan den risk, control, evidence va owner nao.
- Toi da ghi lai it nhat mot cau hoi can tim hieu sau neu con mo ho.

## 8. Outcome lien quan

Trace and protect traffic across firewall, segmentation, DNS, HTTP and TLS boundaries.
