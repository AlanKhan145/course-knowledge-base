## 1. Tom tat

Bai nay giai thich **Authentication** trong boi canh DevSecOps hien dai. Sau bai hoc, ban nen biet topic nay giam risk nao, nam o dau trong lifecycle va co the ap dung vao code, pipeline, cloud, runtime hay incident response.

## 2. Muc tieu hoc tap

- Giai thich duoc Authentication bang ngon ngu cua ban.
- Nhan biet topic nay nam o dau trong DevSecOps lifecycle.
- Tao duoc mot artifact nho: checklist, script, config, scan, detection rule hoac runbook.

## 3. Khai niem chinh

- Authentication tra loi cau hoi ai dang truy cap: user, service, workload hay device.
- Cach lam pho bien gom password, MFA, token, certificate, OAuth va SSO.
- Auth tot can chong credential stuffing, token leak, replay va session hijacking.
