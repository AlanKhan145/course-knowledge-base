## 1. Tom tat

Bai nay giai thich **Defense in Depth Concepts** trong boi canh DevSecOps hien dai. Sau bai hoc, ban nen biet topic nay giam risk nao, nam o dau trong lifecycle va co the ap dung vao code, pipeline, cloud, runtime hay incident response.

## 2. Muc tieu hoc tap

- Giai thich duoc Defense in Depth Concepts bang ngon ngu cua ban.
- Nhan biet topic nay nam o dau trong DevSecOps lifecycle.
- Tao duoc mot artifact nho: checklist, script, config, scan, detection rule hoac runbook.

## 3. Khai niem chinh

- Defense in depth dung nhieu lop bao ve thay vi tin vao mot control duy nhat.
- Lop bao ve gom network, identity, app, data, monitoring, endpoint, CI/CD va cloud policy.
- Moi lop nen co muc tieu ro va khong phu thuoc hoan toan vao lop khac.

## 4. Vi du / Demo

```text
Threat -> vulnerable pattern -> safe pattern -> test case -> pipeline gate
```

## 5. Bai tap thuc hanh

- Viet mot checklist review 5 dong cho topic nay.
- Tao mot test case hoac pseudo-code cho safe pattern.
- Ghi lai pipeline gate nao co the bat loi nay som.

## 6. Loi thuong gap

- Chi sua loi sau khi scan thay vi thiet ke safe pattern tu dau.
- Khong co test case nen loi co the quay lai.
- Bo qua trade-off ve UX, reliability va kha nang van hanh.

## 7. Checklist hoan thanh

- Toi co the giai thich **Defense in Depth Concepts** trong 1-2 phut.
- Toi co mot artifact nho hoac ghi chu thuc hanh cho bai nay.
- Toi biet topic nay lien quan den risk, control, evidence va owner nao.
- Toi da ghi lai it nhat mot cau hoi can tim hieu sau neu con mo ho.

## 8. Outcome lien quan

Design systems with layered controls, zero trust, secure APIs and supply chain protection.

## 9. Tong ket

**Defense in Depth Concepts** la mot moc trong lo trinh DevSecOps. Hay bien no thanh mot checklist, script, config, scan, detection rule, dashboard hoac runbook de kien thuc co cho bam.
