## 1. Tom tat

Bai nay giai thich **SOAR Concepts** trong boi canh DevSecOps hien dai. Sau bai hoc, ban nen biet topic nay giam risk nao, nam o dau trong lifecycle va co the ap dung vao code, pipeline, cloud, runtime hay incident response.

## 2. Muc tieu hoc tap

- Giai thich duoc SOAR Concepts bang ngon ngu cua ban.
- Nhan biet topic nay nam o dau trong DevSecOps lifecycle.
- Tao duoc mot artifact nho: checklist, script, config, scan, detection rule hoac runbook.

## 3. Khai niem chinh

- SOAR tu dong hoa response: enrich alert, tao ticket, block IP, isolate host, notify team va chay playbook.
- Playbook nen co approval gate cho hanh dong nguy hiem.
- Automation tot giam toil nhung khong che mat decision quan trong.

## 4. Vi du / Demo

```text
Alert -> enrich -> create case -> ask approval -> contain -> notify -> close with evidence
```

## 5. Bai tap thuc hanh

- Viet 5 dong tom tat bai hoc khong nhin tai lieu.
- Tao vi du nho bang command, config, script, diagram hoac checklist.
- Tim mot loi production/security co the lien quan va viet cach debug.

## 6. Loi thuong gap

- Hoc thuoc dinh nghia nhung khong tao artifact thuc hanh.
- Bo qua scope, permission va data nhay cam khi dung security tool.
- Khong ghi lai cau hoi con mo de quay lai sau.

## 7. Checklist hoan thanh

- Toi co the giai thich **SOAR Concepts** trong 1-2 phut.
- Toi co mot artifact nho hoac ghi chu thuc hanh cho bai nay.
- Toi biet topic nay lien quan den risk, control, evidence va owner nao.
- Toi da ghi lai it nhat mot cau hoi can tim hieu sau neu con mo ho.

## 8. Outcome lien quan

Automate security response and patching without hiding risk or breaking delivery.

## 9. Tong ket

**SOAR Concepts** la mot moc trong lo trinh DevSecOps. Hay bien no thanh mot checklist, script, config, scan, detection rule, dashboard hoac runbook de kien thuc co cho bam.
