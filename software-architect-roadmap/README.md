## Lo trinh hoc de xuat

### Phase 1 - Foundation (Tuan 1-4)
Hoc section 01 (Understand the Basics) de hieu vai tro, trach nhiem va ky nang can thiet cua mot Software Architect.

### Phase 2 - Programming & Design (Tuan 5-10)
Hoc section 02 (Technical Skills) va 03 (Patterns and Design Principles) de co nen tang ky thuat vung chac.

### Phase 3 - System Architecture (Tuan 11-16)
Hoc section 04 (Architecture) va 05 (APIs and Integrations) de hieu cac kieu kien truc va tich hop he thong.

### Phase 4 - Backend & Data (Tuan 17-20)
Hoc section 06 (Working with Data) va 07 (Web and Mobile) de hieu full-stack architecture.

### Phase 5 - Cloud, DevOps & Security (Tuan 21-26)
Hoc section 08 (Security), 09 (Networks) va 10 (Operations Knowledge) de thiet ke he thong production-ready.

### Phase 6 - Enterprise & Leadership (Tuan 27-32)
Hoc section 11 (Frameworks and Management), 12 (Enterprise Software) va 13 (Relevant Tracks) de tro thanh Architect toan dien.
