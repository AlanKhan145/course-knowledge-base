## 1. Tom tat

Bai nay giai thich **EMC DMS IBM BPM** trong boi canh Software Architecture hien dai. Sau bai hoc, ban nen nam khai niem, biet vi sao no quan trong doi voi Architect va co mot bai tap nho de ap dung.

EMC (Dell Technologies) cung cap Enterprise Content Management (ECM) cho quan ly tai lieu. IBM BPM (Business Process Manager) cho tu dong hoa quy trinh nghiep vu.

## 2. Muc tieu hoc tap

- Giai thich duoc **EMC DMS IBM BPM** bang ngon ngu cua ban trong 1-2 phut.
- Nhan biet khi nao kien thuc nay anh huong den quyet dinh kien truc.
- Thuc hanh vi du nho va ghi lai cac diem can luu y.

## 3. Khai niem chinh

- EMC Documentum: ECM platform cho luu tru, quan ly, workflow tai lieu
- IBM BPM (Business Automation Workflow): tu dong hoa quy trinh phuc tap
- IBM FileNet Content Manager: ECM va workflow cho enterprise
- Thich hop trong nganh tai chinh, bao hiem, chinh phu

## 6. Loi thuong gap

- Hoc thuoc dinh nghia nhung khong biet ap dung vao bai toan thuc te.
- Bo qua trade-off: moi giai phap deu co diem manh va diem yeu.
- Ap dung may moc ma khong can nhac den context cu the cua he thong.
