## 1. Tom tat

Bai nay giai thich **NoSQL Databases** trong boi canh Software Architecture hien dai. Sau bai hoc, ban nen nam khai niem, biet vi sao no quan trong doi voi Architect va co mot bai tap nho de ap dung.

NoSQL databases (MongoDB, Cassandra, DynamoDB, Redis) linh hoat hon SQL ve schema, scale tot hon theo chieu ngang (horizontal).

## 2. Muc tieu hoc tap

- Giai thich duoc **NoSQL Databases** bang ngon ngu cua ban trong 1-2 phut.
- Nhan biet khi nao kien thuc nay anh huong den quyet dinh kien truc.
- Thuc hanh vi du nho va ghi lai cac diem can luu y.

## 3. Khai niem chinh

- Document DB: MongoDB, CouchDB cho du lieu semi-structured
- Key-Value: Redis, DynamoDB cho high-speed lookup
- Column-family: Cassandra, HBase cho write-heavy workloads
- Graph DB: Neo4j cho du lieu co nhieu quan he phuc tap
