## 1. Tom tat

Bai nay giai thich **Hashing Algorithms** trong boi canh Software Architecture hien dai. Sau bai hoc, ban nen nam khai niem, biet vi sao no quan trong doi voi Architect va co mot bai tap nho de ap dung.

Hash function bien doi du lieu dau vao thanh chuoi co do dai co dinh. Quan trong trong luu tru mat khau, xac minh tinh toan ven cua du lieu.

## 2. Muc tieu hoc tap

- Giai thich duoc **Hashing Algorithms** bang ngon ngu cua ban trong 1-2 phut.
- Nhan biet khi nao kien thuc nay anh huong den quyet dinh kien truc.
- Thuc hanh vi du nho va ghi lai cac diem can luu y.

## 3. Khai niem chinh

- MD5, SHA-1 da bi broken, khong dung cho bao mat
- SHA-256, SHA-3 an toan cho checksum va digital signature
- bcrypt, scrypt, Argon2 duoc thiet ke cho password hashing (co salt, cost factor)
- Hash la one-way, khac voi encryption (two-way)
