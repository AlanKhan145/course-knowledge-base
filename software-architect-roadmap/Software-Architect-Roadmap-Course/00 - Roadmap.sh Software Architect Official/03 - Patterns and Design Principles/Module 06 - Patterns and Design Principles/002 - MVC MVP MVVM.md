## 1. Tom tat

Bai nay giai thich **MVC MVP MVVM** trong boi canh Software Architecture hien dai. Sau bai hoc, ban nen nam khai niem, biet vi sao no quan trong doi voi Architect va co mot bai tap nho de ap dung.

Cac pattern phan tach giao dien nguoi dung khoi logic nghiep vu: MVC cho web, MVP cho Android truyen thong, MVVM cho reactive UI.

## 2. Muc tieu hoc tap

- Giai thich duoc **MVC MVP MVVM** bang ngon ngu cua ban trong 1-2 phut.
- Nhan biet khi nao kien thuc nay anh huong den quyet dinh kien truc.
- Thuc hanh vi du nho va ghi lai cac diem can luu y.

## 3. Khai niem chinh

- MVC: Model (data), View (UI), Controller (logic) - cuc ky pho bien trong web
- MVP: Model, View (passive), Presenter (logic) - testable hon MVC
- MVVM: Model, View, ViewModel (bindable) - ideal cho reactive/declarative UI
- Tat ca deu giai quyet separation of concerns, testability

## 5. Bai tap thuc hanh

- Viet 5 dong tom tat bai hoc nay khong nhin tai lieu.
- Tim mot he thong thuc te (Google, Netflix, Amazon) su dung khai niem nay va ghi lai cach ho ap dung.
- Tao vi du nho hoac ve so do mo ta khai niem.

## 6. Loi thuong gap

- Hoc thuoc dinh nghia nhung khong biet ap dung vao bai toan thuc te.
- Bo qua trade-off: moi giai phap deu co diem manh va diem yeu.
- Ap dung may moc ma khong can nhac den context cu the cua he thong.

## 7. Checklist hoan thanh

- Toi co the giai thich **MVC MVP MVVM** trong 1-2 phut khong can nhin tai lieu.
- Toi biet it nhat mot truong hop su dung thuc te.
- Toi hieu cac trade-off chinh khi ap dung khai niem nay.
- Toi da ghi lai it nhat mot cau hoi can tim hieu sau neu con mo ho.

## 8. Tong ket

**MVC MVP MVVM** la mot kiem kien thuc quan trong trong hanh trang cua Software Architect. Hay bien no thanh vi du cu the, so do hoac checklist de kien thuc co cho bam vung trong thuc te.
