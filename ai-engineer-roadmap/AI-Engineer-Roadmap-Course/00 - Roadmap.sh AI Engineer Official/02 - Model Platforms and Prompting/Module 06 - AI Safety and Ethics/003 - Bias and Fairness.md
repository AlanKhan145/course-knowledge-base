## 3. Khai niem chinh

- Bias and Fairness giam risk khi AI app gap user input, du lieu rieng hoac tool co quyen.
- Can thiet ke guardrail theo layer: input, retrieval, tool permission, output va monitoring.
- Safety khong phai mot buoc cuoi, ma la phan cua design va regression testing.
