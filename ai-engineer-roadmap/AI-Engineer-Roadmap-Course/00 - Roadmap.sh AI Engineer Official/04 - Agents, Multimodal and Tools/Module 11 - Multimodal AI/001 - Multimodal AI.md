## 3. Khai niem chinh

- Multimodal AI works across text, images, audio, speech, video or documents.
- It enables study assistants, document extraction, image QA, speech interfaces and media analysis.
- Each modality adds data quality, privacy and evaluation challenges.

## 4. Vi du / Demo

