## 2. Muc tieu hoc tap

- Tra loi cau hoi ngan gon, ro y va khong noi qua kha nang.
- Gan cau tra loi voi project hoac kinh nghiem thuc hanh.
- Neu cau hoi co nhieu cach hieu, biet dat lai pham vi va neu trade-off.

## 3. Khai niem chinh

- Dung cau hoi nay de luyen cach giai thich kinh nghiem, trade-off va lo trinh hoc full-stack.
- Cau tra loi tot nen co vi du project, biet gioi han cua ban va noi ro cach ban hoc tiep.
- Khi phong van, uu tien cach suy nghi, debug va giao tiep hon la chi liet ke tu khoa.

- Tra loi qua chung chung nhu doc lai dinh nghia.

- Toi co the giai thich **What does a Full Stack Developer job description typically look like?** trong 1-2 phut.
