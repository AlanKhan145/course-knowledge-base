## 1. Tom tat

Bai nay giai thich **dependency management** trong boi canh full-stack hien dai. Sau bai hoc, ban nen biet no nam o dau trong hanh trinh tu frontend den backend, database va deployment.

## 2. Muc tieu hoc tap

- Giai thich duoc dependency management bang ngon ngu cua ban.
- Nhan biet kien thuc nay nam o frontend, backend, database, cloud hay workflow.
- Ap dung vao mot vi du nho trong full-stack project.

## 3. Khai niem chinh

- Dependency management la viec chon, cap nhat, khoa version va go bo package co chu dich.
- Can theo doi breaking changes, security advisory va transitive dependency.
- Mot project tot co lenh audit/update va policy nang cap dinh ky.

- Toi co the giai thich **dependency management** trong 1-2 phut.
