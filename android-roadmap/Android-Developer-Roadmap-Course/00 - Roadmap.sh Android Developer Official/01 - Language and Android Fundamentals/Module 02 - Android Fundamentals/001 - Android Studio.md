## 1. Tom tat

Bai nay giai thich **Android Studio** trong boi canh Android Developer Roadmap 2026. Sau bai hoc, ban nen biet topic nay nam o dau trong app Android, lien quan den UI, lifecycle, state, data, network, quality hoac release nhu the nao va co the bien thanh artifact trong portfolio.

## 2. Muc tieu hoc tap

- Giai thich duoc Android Studio bang ngon ngu cua ban.
- Nhan biet topic nay anh huong den UX, do on dinh, maintainability, performance hoac release risk nao.
- Ap dung vao mot app nho bang code, diagram, test, checklist, screenshot hoac README.

## 3. Khai niem chinh

- Android Studio is the official IDE for building, running, profiling and debugging Android apps.
- Learn the project wizard, emulator/device setup, Logcat, debugger, layout tools and Gradle sync behavior.
- Most Android productivity comes from knowing where build, runtime and device errors appear.
