## 1. Tom tat

Bai nay giai thich **Kotlin** trong boi canh Android Developer Roadmap 2026. Sau bai hoc, ban nen biet topic nay nam o dau trong app Android, lien quan den UI, lifecycle, state, data, network, quality hoac release nhu the nao va co the bien thanh artifact trong portfolio.

## 2. Muc tieu hoc tap

- Giai thich duoc Kotlin bang ngon ngu cua ban.
- Nhan biet topic nay anh huong den UX, do on dinh, maintainability, performance hoac release risk nao.
- Ap dung vao mot app nho bang code, diagram, test, checklist, screenshot hoac README.

## 3. Khai niem chinh

- Kotlin is the primary language for modern Android development.
- It works naturally with Jetpack Compose, Coroutines, Flow and the Android Kotlin extensions ecosystem.
- Focus on null safety, data classes, sealed classes, extension functions and readable functional style.
