## 1. Tom tat

Bai nay giai thich **Google Play Console** trong boi canh Android Developer Roadmap 2026. Sau bai hoc, ban nen biet topic nay nam o dau trong app Android, lien quan den UI, lifecycle, state, data, network, quality hoac release nhu the nao va co the bien thanh artifact trong portfolio.

## 2. Muc tieu hoc tap

- Giai thich duoc Google Play Console bang ngon ngu cua ban.
- Nhan biet topic nay anh huong den UX, do on dinh, maintainability, performance hoac release risk nao.
- Ap dung vao mot app nho bang code, diagram, test, checklist, screenshot hoac README.

## 3. Khai niem chinh

- Google Play Console is used to manage store listings, testing tracks, releases and production rollout.
- A release requires signing, versioning, privacy information, screenshots, metadata and review readiness.
- Internal testing and staged rollout reduce risk before full production release.

## 4. Thuc hanh

2. Write goal, features, run steps, screenshots and known limitations.
3. Link the artifact from the course progress tracker.

## 5. Bai tap

Create the portfolio deliverable and add a README section explaining how to run or review it.
