## 1. Tom tat

Bai nay giai thich **ML Engineer vs AI Engineer** trong boi canh Machine Learning Engineer Roadmap 2026. Sau bai hoc, ban nen biet topic nay nam o dau trong quy trinh ML, lien quan den data, feature, model, training, evaluation, deployment hoac portfolio nhu the nao.

## 2. Muc tieu hoc tap

- Giai thich duoc ML Engineer vs AI Engineer bang ngon ngu cua ban.
- Nhan biet topic nay anh huong den model quality, generalization, interpretability, cost hoac production risk nao.
- Ap dung vao mot artifact nho: notebook, script, metric table, chart, diagram, model report hoac README.

## 3. Khai niem chinh

- ML Engineers focus heavily on data, training, evaluation, optimization and the model lifecycle.
- AI Engineers often focus more broadly on LLM apps, agents, APIs, automation and AI product integration.
- The roles overlap, but this roadmap emphasizes model building and evaluation fundamentals.
